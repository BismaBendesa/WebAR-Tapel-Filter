The Protector Character : Tapel Barong

Detailed Description

Name Red Tapel Barong (Tapel Barong Merah)
Color Theme Dominantly red on the face, symbolizing energy, protection, and fiery spirit.
Type Of Tapel Can appear in Barong Ket, Barong Bangkung (pig), or ritual variants with red coloration.
Material Usually pule wood (holy tree), decorated with gold leaf, mirrors, hair/fur, shells, and fabric ornaments.
Facial Features
  • - Large, round eyes with red outline
  • - Prominent fangs or teeth
  • - Wide, protective smile or fierce expression
  • - Red facial paint for intensity
Symbolism Represents strength, spiritual heat (taksu), courage, purification, and protection against dark forces.
Use In Rituals Used in Barong dance, Calonarang, temple ceremonies, and purification rituals (ngatep, pangelukatan).
Spritual Status Considered sacred (keramat), stored in temple shrines, treated with offerings, and handled only by trained caretakers.
Movement Style Dynamic, expressive movements symbolizing vigilance and power.
Performance Acts as a protector character, balancing the negative forces (e.g., Rangda).
